School

We all have had to face the challenges with managing our finances and money as adults solely because we did not receive the right money management lessons at the right time as adolescents. An essential life skill like money management can go a long way in building savings-conscience among the youth and thus allow them to nurture an awareness of their spending habits. Â 
In recent years government, regulators, educators, financial experts and social change makers have realised the significance of financial literacy and are now encouraging provision of financial literacy for children across schools. Thereby, financial literacy and money management skills are being introduced as key life skill in academic curriculum. and hence our government has begun this life skill in their academic curriculum which will nurture them from an early age and create a strong foundation. This has resulted in the Introduction of the Summer School of Financial Literacy by Finsaarthi Learning Solutions. The curriculum for the program has been made by experienced industry veterans with subject-matter expertise.

1. Who are the participants:

All boards school students from the private and government sector from 8th Standard onwards.


2. Mode of Training:

Offline and Online (Blended Learning).


3. Duration:

10 hours (We can customize this entire course and duration as per the requirement).


4. Curriculum:

We cover the following topics for the students.
1. Saving, Expenditure and Investment Basics
2. Improving decision-making skills
3. Fighting Inflation & Gratification
4. Importance of Financial Planning
5. Budgeting and Financial Responsibility
6. How to create a pocket money budget and save money aside
7. Different finance and banking intermediaries and their function
8. Role of different regulators
9. Money Management Principles
10. Different Investment Products (Old and New)


5. Pedagogy:

We make learning more interesting by introducing our unique Gamification ideas in the curriculum. We will be using the following pedagogy for the same.
1. Real-time Quiz
2. Presentation
3. Gamification Learning
4. Live Case Study


6. Outcome:

After going through the detailed course and well-designed curriculum children will be able to understand the importance of saving and the adverse effect of unnecessary spending and thus help them to reduce their gratification. They will be more focused on their career and start working towards their financial goals which will reduce the unwanted expenditures of their parents and thus build a strong foundation for wealth creation at an early age.


7. Success Stories:

Our trainers and subject matter experts have conducted sessions for 100 plus schools educating 20000 plus students across India covering government schools, CBSE board, ICSE board, IGCSE schools and state board schools.Â 


College

Post Covid our country is seeing tremendous opportunities and growth which is helping us to brand globally and move towards robust development in all areas of the world economy. Introduction to the startup culture and an increasing number of unicorns have made it compulsory for an individual to be more responsible and accountable towards money and its management. Thus Financial Literacy at a young age helps an individual to move ahead and progress in life as an informed investor who has the ability to take his own financial decisions and learn the art of wealth creation.
College days are the time when we really understand the importance of money and we try to save as much as we can from the pocket money we get. Most of us get our first income during this time and the wheel of earning starts from this age. This is the only time when an individual can start his or her wealth creation process or start spending on all the unwanted materialistic things and creating their own pitfall of debt. Finsaarthi Learning Solutions would like to help each and every individual to understand the wealth creation process and develop financial money management skills at a young age by the means of Financial Literacy Programs.

1. Who are the participants:

College Students (11th Grade onwards to Post graduate)


2. Mode of Training:

Offline and Online (Blended Learning).


3. Duration:

Minimum 1 hour (We can customize this entire course and duration as per the College and Naac requirement of 30 hours on various topics related to finance)


4. Curriculum:

We cover the following topics for the College students
1. Saving, Expenditure and Investment Basics
2. Introduction to the Financial Markets, Capital Markets and Money Markets
3. Mutual Funds and Stock market
4. Managing Risk through Insurance (Life, Health and General)
5. Government Securities, Pension Products and Retirement Planning
6. Financial Goal Planning, Risk Management, Investment Planning, Tax Planning & Estate Planning
7. Wealth management and Investment banking
8. Banking and Non banking Products
9. Asset Allocation Techniques & Emerging Financial Products
10. Fintech


5. Pedagogy:

We make the learning more interesting by introducing our unique Gamification ideas in the curriculum. We will be using the following pedagogy for the same.
1. Real-time Quiz
2. Presentation
3. Gamification Learning
4. Live Case Study
5. Certification


6. Outcome:

After going through the modules students will be able to understand the importance of money management and take the first step towards wealth creation process by being financially literate.


7. Success Stories:

Our trainers and subject matter experts have conducted sessions for 300 plus colleges educating 30000 plus students across India covering junior college, undergraduate and postgraduate college.
